Hello everyone! I am Pamela Navarro. I am 19 years old and I live in Barra Opol, Misamis Oriental. I am the eldest in the family, and my hobbies when I have free time are reading, watching movies, and sleeping. I don't know if sleeping is included in what so-called hobbies are, but let's include it. My dream is to learn as many instruments as possible. It's fascinating to me that instruments convey a lot of emotions and ideas, so if given a chance, why not?. As I grew older, I dreamed of visiting many different places and trying things that I hadn't done before. Back then, I used to fantasize about living in Paris and getting my life together. Once I'm financially free, I would like to fulfill my childhood dream.

My teenage years were like a roller coaster experience. As a normal teen, I sometimes break the rules to feel what it is like to have fun outside of your parents' guidance. However, as I grew older and felt like I was about to quit my teenage life, somehow I realized that I enjoyed it, and that will forever be engraved in my heart.
